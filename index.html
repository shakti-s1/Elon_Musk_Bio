Elon Musk

Early life

As a child musk was bullied a lot. In one incident, after calling a boy whose father had committed suicide "stupid", Musk was severely beaten and thrown down concrete steps.At age ten, he developed an interest in computing and video games, teaching himself how to program from the VIC-20 user manual.


Musk's family was wealthy during his youth. His father was elected to the Pretoria City Council as a representative of the anti-apartheid Progressive Party and has said that his children shared their father's dislike of apartheid.His maternal grandfather and namesake, Joshua Elon Haldeman, was an American-born Canadian who took his family on record-breaking journeys to Africa and Australia in a single-engine Bellanca airplane.Haldeman was a member of the Social Credit Party of Canada, possessed antisemitic beliefs, and supported the Technocracy movement.After his parents divorced in 1980, Musk chose to live primarily with his father. Musk later regretted his decision and became estranged from his father. He has a paternal half-sister and a half-brother.

Business Career

Tesla

  • Elon Musk is the co-founder and CEO of Tesla, an electric vehicle (EV) and clean energy company.

  • He famously receives a salary of just $1 per year as CEO.

  • Musk's fascination with electric cars was sparked by the 2008 Tesla Roadster, the first production car with a lithium-ion battery. Tesla's Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y have become popular EVs under his leadership.

  • He played a pivotal role in transforming Tesla from a niche EV startup to a major player in the automotive industry. Musk's ambitious vision includes making sustainable transportation accessible to all.
Personal Life
  1. Elon Musk is known for his intense work ethic, often working long hours and juggling multiple companies.

  2. He's known for his interest in science fiction and has named SpaceX rockets after ships from the "Culture" series by Iain M. Banks.

  3. Musk briefly overtook Jeff Bezos as the world's richest person in early 2021, largely due to the success of Tesla's stock.

  4. Musk has a passion for innovation and has expressed concerns about the potential dangers of artificial intelligence, prompting his involvement in AI safety research.
